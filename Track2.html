Track 2 Overview

As audio-video generation becomes increasingly realistic, mitigating malicious misuse has become a key research priority. To raise awareness and accelerate defenses, we present a General AIGC Audio-Vision Detection Challenge, supported by a large-scale, high-quality multimodal dataset covering 25 generation techniques and three forgery modes to advance robust audio-visual detection.

Evaluation Metrics

Task Components

  • Real: The video is real, which means there is no fake segment in the video.
  • Fake: The video is fake, which means there is at least one fake segment in the video.
  • Detection: The metrics for this task is the AUC score.
  • Localization: The metrics for this task are AP (Average Precision) and AR (Average Recall).
  • The Final score is the average of the above two scores.
